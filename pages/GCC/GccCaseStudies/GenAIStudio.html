Overview

This case highlights how a global fintech enterprise transformed its GenAI vision into a production-grade innovation studio within 90 days — delivering measurable impact through rapid execution, scalable architecture, and AI-ready teams.

Challenge

The client's GenAI strategy was stalled at the planning stage, with no scalable execution roadmap. Internal teams lacked the LLMOps expertise to move from experimentation to production, and GenAI investments risked becoming sunk costs without a clear path to value.

Solution

Parkar launched a fully operational GenAI Studio in 90 days, including:

  • Production-grade pipelines using LangChain and Pinecone
  • Azure OpenAI integration for secure enterprise deployment
  • Upskilling 50+ engineers in LLMOps and GenAI practices

Key Results

  • 3x faster time-to-value on AI use cases
  • 20% increase in retention among upskilled talent
  • Full studio transitioned to internal teams with zero disruption
