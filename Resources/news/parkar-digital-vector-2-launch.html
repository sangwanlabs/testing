Parkar Digital launches its flagship Observability product Vector 2.0

Resources / News / Parkar Digital Vector 2.0 Launch

This will empower organizations with unified observability and AI-powered monitoring

Bengaluru, May 7 -- Parkar Digital, a leading technology solutions company, today announced the launch of Vector 2.0, its flagship observability product designed to empower organizations with unified observability and AI-powered monitoring capabilities. Vector 2.0 represents a major leap forward in how enterprises monitor, diagnose, and optimize their technology infrastructure and applications.

Vector 2.0 introduces a comprehensive suite of features built to address the growing complexity of modern IT environments:

  • Unified observability -- A single pane of glass across infrastructure, applications, networks, and user experience, eliminating data silos and providing end-to-end visibility.
  • Distributed AI-powered monitoring -- Intelligent agents deployed across distributed environments that leverage artificial intelligence to detect anomalies and surface insights in real time.
  • APM and Network Monitoring -- Deep application performance monitoring combined with network-level telemetry for complete stack coverage.
  • Infrastructure Monitoring with MLOps -- Continuous monitoring of infrastructure health integrated with MLOps pipelines to ensure model performance and reliability in production.
  • AI-driven anomaly detection -- Machine learning algorithms that establish dynamic baselines and automatically identify deviations, reducing alert fatigue and accelerating incident detection.
  • Automated root cause analysis -- Intelligent correlation of events across services and infrastructure layers to pinpoint the origin of issues within seconds, not hours.
  • Customizable dashboards -- Flexible, role-based dashboards that allow teams from engineering to executive leadership to visualize the metrics that matter most to them.

"Vector 2.0 is the culmination of years of research, engineering, and deep customer engagement. We built this platform to solve the fundamental challenge that enterprises face today -- the inability to see, understand, and act on what is happening across their increasingly complex technology estates. With Vector 2.0, we are giving organizations the power to move from reactive firefighting to proactive, intelligence-driven operations."

-- Gaurav Singh, CEO, Parkar Digital

"From an engineering perspective, Vector 2.0 represents a ground-up rethinking of how observability data is collected, processed, and analyzed. We have built a highly scalable, cloud-native architecture that can handle millions of data points per second while delivering sub-second query performance. The AI layer is not a bolt-on -- it is deeply integrated into the platform's core, enabling the kind of real-time intelligence that modern enterprises demand."

-- Amit Gandhi, CTO, Parkar Digital

As part of the Vector 2.0 launch, Parkar Digital is also investing in workforce training and enablement programs to ensure that customers can maximize the value of the platform from day one. Dedicated onboarding specialists, comprehensive documentation, and hands-on workshops will be available to help IT teams across industries adopt unified observability practices and integrate Vector 2.0 seamlessly into their existing workflows.
